FAILURE MODE AND EFFECTS ANALYSIS (FMEA)
Document No: FMEA-001
Version: 1.0
Machines Covered: CNC-M01, HYD-P02, CVB-003
Department: Maintenance Engineering

=========================================================
PURPOSE
=========================================================
This FMEA identifies potential failure modes for each machine,
their effects, causes, and recommended controls. Used by
maintenance engineers for risk-based maintenance planning.

Risk Priority Number (RPN) = Severity × Occurrence × Detection
  Severity:   1 (negligible) to 10 (catastrophic)
  Occurrence: 1 (rare) to 10 (frequent)
  Detection:  1 (always detected) to 10 (never detected)
RPN > 100 requires immediate corrective action.

=========================================================
SECTION 1: CNC MILLING MACHINE (CNC-M01)
=========================================================

SUBSYSTEM: Spindle Assembly
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Spindle Bearing Failure
  Effect: Machine stops, tool breakage risk, part scrap
  Cause: Inadequate lubrication, overload, end of bearing life
  Severity: 8  Occurrence: 3  Detection: 4  RPN: 96
  Current Controls: Vibration monitoring, temperature sensor, periodic bearing replacement
  Recommended Action: Reduce PM interval to 1800h, add acoustic emission sensor
  Error Code: E08

Failure Mode: Spindle Motor Overload
  Effect: Auto-shutdown, production loss
  Cause: Dull tool, incorrect cutting parameters, chip packing
  Severity: 6  Occurrence: 5  Detection: 2  RPN: 60
  Current Controls: Motor current monitoring, operator training
  Error Code: E01

SUBSYSTEM: Axis Drive System
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Servo Drive Fault
  Effect: Axis stops, machine halts mid-cycle
  Cause: Overheating, encoder fault, power fluctuation
  Severity: 7  Occurrence: 3  Detection: 3  RPN: 63
  Current Controls: Drive fault alarms, encoder cable check in PM
  Error Code: E02

Failure Mode: Linear Guideway Wear
  Effect: Poor surface finish, dimensional inaccuracy
  Cause: Inadequate lubrication, abrasive chips
  Severity: 6  Occurrence: 2  Detection: 6  RPN: 72
  Current Controls: Auto-lubrication system, periodic backlash check
  Recommended Action: Thermal imaging of guideways during C-level PM

SUBSYSTEM: Coolant System
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Coolant Pump Failure
  Effect: Tool overheating, fire risk, surface finish degradation
  Cause: Blocked strainer, cavitation, impeller wear
  Severity: 7  Occurrence: 3  Detection: 2  RPN: 42
  Current Controls: Coolant flow sensor, low coolant alarm
  Error Code: E03

Failure Mode: Coolant Concentration Drift
  Effect: Corrosion, bacterial growth, skin sensitization
  Cause: Evaporation, water addition without concentration check
  Severity: 5  Occurrence: 6  Detection: 5  RPN: 150  ← ACTION REQUIRED
  Current Controls: Monthly concentration check
  Recommended Action: Install inline refractometer, weekly check

=========================================================
SECTION 2: HYDRAULIC PUMP UNIT (HYD-P02)
=========================================================

SUBSYSTEM: Hydraulic Pump
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Pump Cavitation
  Effect: Rapid pump wear, noise, pressure instability
  Cause: Low fluid level, blocked suction, cold oil viscosity
  Severity: 8  Occurrence: 4  Detection: 3  RPN: 96
  Current Controls: Low level alarm, suction strainer
  Error Code: E16

Failure Mode: Pump Internal Leakage (Bypass)
  Effect: Reduced flow, slow actuator movement, overheating
  Cause: Pump wear, contaminated fluid
  Severity: 6  Occurrence: 3  Detection: 5  RPN: 90
  Current Controls: Quarterly flow rate check, fluid analysis
  Recommended Action: Track pump volumetric efficiency monthly

SUBSYSTEM: Pressure Relief Valve
─────────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: PRV Stuck Closed
  Effect: CATASTROPHIC overpressure, hose burst risk
  Cause: Debris in seat, corrosion, long idle period
  Severity: 10  Occurrence: 2  Detection: 3  RPN: 60
  Current Controls: PRV manual lift test weekly, pressure alarm
  Error Code: E11

Failure Mode: PRV Stuck Open (Passing)
  Effect: Cannot reach operating pressure, slow cycle times
  Cause: Contamination in seat, spring fatigue
  Severity: 5  Occurrence: 3  Detection: 2  RPN: 30
  Current Controls: Pressure monitoring

SUBSYSTEM: Hydraulic Hoses
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Hose Burst (Catastrophic)
  Effect: FATAL fluid injection injury risk, fire hazard
  Cause: Abrasion, fatigue, pressure spike, age (>4 years)
  Severity: 10  Occurrence: 2  Detection: 6  RPN: 120  ← ACTION REQUIRED
  Current Controls: Visual hose inspection, age-based replacement
  Recommended Action: Replace all hoses at 4-year interval regardless of condition
  Error Code: E20

SUBSYSTEM: Fluid Quality
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Fluid Contamination (Particle)
  Effect: Valve spool wear, pump wear, seal damage
  Cause: Ingress via open reservoir, dirty filter, system wear
  Severity: 7  Occurrence: 4  Detection: 4  RPN: 112  ← ACTION REQUIRED
  Current Controls: Return filter 10 micron, quarterly fluid analysis
  Recommended Action: Install offline filtration unit (kidney loop)

=========================================================
SECTION 3: CONVEYOR BELT SYSTEM (CVB-003)
=========================================================

SUBSYSTEM: Belt
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Belt Misalignment (Tracking Off)
  Effect: Belt edge damage, spillage, potential belt tear
  Cause: Off-center loading, frame misalignment, worn tracking guides
  Severity: 5  Occurrence: 6  Detection: 2  RPN: 60
  Current Controls: Tracking sensor, operator visual check
  Error Code: E23

Failure Mode: Belt Slip on Drive Pulley
  Effect: Reduced throughput, belt overheating
  Cause: Low belt tension, worn lagging, overloading
  Severity: 5  Occurrence: 4  Detection: 3  RPN: 60
  Current Controls: Speed sensor (slip detection), tension check in PM
  Error Code: E21

Failure Mode: Belt Tear (Catastrophic)
  Effect: Complete production stoppage, debris in product
  Cause: Sharp object on belt, splice failure, foreign material
  Severity: 9  Occurrence: 2  Detection: 5  RPN: 90
  Current Controls: Belt tear detector, visual inspection
  Error Code: E29

SUBSYSTEM: Drive System
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Gearbox Bearing Failure
  Effect: Machine stoppage, extensive repair cost
  Cause: Lubrication failure, overloading, end of life
  Severity: 8  Occurrence: 2  Detection: 5  RPN: 80
  Current Controls: Oil level check, vibration monitoring
  Error Code: E25

Failure Mode: Drive Motor Overload
  Effect: Motor trips, production stops
  Cause: Mechanical jam, belt overloaded, motor winding fault
  Severity: 6  Occurrence: 4  Detection: 2  RPN: 48
  Current Controls: Motor overload relay, current monitoring
  Error Code: E22

SUBSYSTEM: Safety Systems
─────────────────────────────
Failure Mode: Emergency Pull Cord Failure (Does Not Stop Belt)
  Effect: CATASTROPHIC — operator entanglement, fatal injury
  Cause: Cord slack, corroded switch, wiring fault
  Severity: 10  Occurrence: 1  Detection: 2  RPN: 20
  Current Controls: Daily pull cord test, monthly switch inspection
  Recommended Action: Pull cord test must be logged every shift — mandatory

=========================================================
FMEA SUMMARY — HIGH RPN ITEMS (Action Required)
=========================================================
RPN > 100:
  1. CNC-M01 Coolant Concentration Drift  RPN=150 → Install refractometer
  2. HYD-P02 Hose Burst                   RPN=120 → 4-year mandatory replacement
  3. HYD-P02 Fluid Contamination          RPN=112 → Add offline filtration

RPN 90-100 (Monitor closely):
  4. CNC-M01 Spindle Bearing Failure      RPN=96
  5. HYD-P02 Pump Cavitation              RPN=96
  6. CVB-003 Belt Tear                    RPN=90
